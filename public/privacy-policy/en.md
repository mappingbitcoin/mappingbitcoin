# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 18, 2025_

MappingBitcoin.com (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our website.

## 1. Information We Collect

- **IP address**: Processed **locally on your device** to infer approximate location for content and currency display. We do **not** store or transmit this information to our servers.
- **Cookies**: Google Analytics uses cookies to collect usage data (browser, device, session duration). No personally identifiable data is stored.

We do **not** collect names, emails, or other personal data directly.

## 2. Third‑Party Data

We use location data and venue information sourced from the **OpenStreetMap** community. The contributor data is managed under their respective privacy policies:
- OpenStreetMap itself

We do **not** store your submitted form data ourselves; it is posted to OSM as public data as you confirm.

## 3. Google Analytics

We use **Google Analytics** for website improvement. Your data is anonymized and aggregated.  
You can opt out by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).

## 4. Cookies

No cookies are used beyond Google Analytics. We don’t set any tracking cookies of our own.

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your data. Only anonymized usage metrics are seen by Google Analytics.  
Venue and location data is public via OpenStreetMap and our applications.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your location (EU, CA, Brazil, Vietnam, etc.), you may have the right to:
- Access, rectify, or delete data
- Object to processing
- Lodge a complaint with supervisory authority

To exercise any rights, email us at [satoshi@mappingbitcoin.com].

## 7. Contact

Questions? Contact us at [satoshi@mappingbitcoin.com].
